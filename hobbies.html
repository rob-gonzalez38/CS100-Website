My Hobbies

Here are some hobbies that are a big part of my life. Each one gives me a different way to enjoy my free time, stay active, and connect with others.

Hobby Why I Enjoy It
Riding Motorcycles I love the freedom and focus it brings. It’s both exciting and calming, and it gives me a chance to clear my head on the open road.
Pokemon Go It combines gaming with being outdoors. I enjoy exploring new areas, catching Pokemon, and playing with friends.
Hiking Hiking lets me recharge while staying active. Idaho’s trails give me the chance to enjoy nature and challenge myself physically.
Spending Time with Friends Friends keep me grounded and make life more enjoyable. Whether it’s hanging out, riding motorcycles, or just catching up, I value the memories and support we share.
